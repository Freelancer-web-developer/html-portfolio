How much does it cost?

Transportation

  • Kathmandu to Pokhara (tourist bus):
    • NPR 700–1,000 (USD 5–8)
  • Pokhara to Kande (shared jeep/taxi):
    • NPR 2,000–3,000 (USD 15–23)
  • Round-trip total:
    • NPR 3,500–4,500 (USD 25–35)

Accommodation Costs (Teahouse Lodging)

  • Budget teahouses:
    • NPR 300–1,000 per night (USD 3–8)
  • Higher elevations (around 3,500m/11,500ft):
    • NPR 500–1,000 per night (USD 5–8)
  • Total for 5–7 nights:
    • NPR 1,500–7,000 (USD 12–50)

Meals (Teahouse Dining)

  • Typical meal (dal bhat, noodles, rice dishes):
    • NPR 300–1,000 per meal (USD 3–8)
  • Daily meals (3 meals):
    • NPR 1,500–3,000 (USD 12–23)
  • Total for 5–7 days:
    • NPR 7,500–21,000 (USD 60–160)

Miscellaneous Costs

  • Water:
    • NPR 100–200 per bottle (USD 1–2)
  • Charging Devices:
    • NPR 100–300 per hour (USD 1–2)
  • Hot Showers:
    • NPR 200–500 per shower (USD 2–4)

Total Estimated Costs Is USD 145–235 Per Person

  • Transportation (round-trip): USD 25–35
  • Accommodation (5–7 nights): USD 50–80
  • Meals (5–7 days): USD 60–100
  • Miscellaneous (Water, Charging, Showers): USD 10–20
