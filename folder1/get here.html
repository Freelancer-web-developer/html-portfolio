How to get here >

Travelling from Kathmandu:

  • Begin your journey to Mardi Himal by traveling from Kathmandu to Pokhara.
  • You can take a regular bus or a tourist bus, which typically takes 6–8 hours.
  • Alternatively, you can choose a quick 25-minute flight.
  • Pokhara, situated at around 800 meters, serves as the gateway to many trekking routes in the Annapurna region.

Travelling from Pokhara:

  • Once in Pokhara, you’ll need to select a starting point for your trek—Phedi, Kande, Dhampus, or Sidding.
  • Each starting point is about an hour’s drive from the city, providing easy access to the trail.
  • The trail gradually climbs through lush forests, charming villages, and scenic ridgelines.
  • If you take the Dhampus trail:
    • Begin with a gradual ascent to Pitam Deurali.
    • Continue to Low Camp and stay overnight.
    • Proceed the next day to High Camp and stay overnight.
    • Wake up around 4 AM to watch the sunrise from the viewing point.
    • Finally, reach the breathtaking viewing point at 4,200 meters.
